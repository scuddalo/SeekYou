What makes SeekYou different



Here's what SeekYou isn't: Loopt, Gowalla, Rally Up, AroundMe, Dopplr, Foursquare, Brightkite, Latitude, Poynt, Scvngr, Facebook Places, WeePlaces, or any of the other casual Location-Based Services applications. SeekYou is a mobile enterprise application designed by professionals, for professionals.

SeekYou works with your affiliations -- your alumni associations, your professional associations, your company or organization's employee list, your client list -- to provide you with a meaningful database of people who are near your location. It gives you the opportunity to see and connect with the people around you who are important to you. It gives you the opportunity to maintain your critical professional relationships. It gives you the opportunities that you would otherwise miss.

In fact, the event that started SeekYou took place at London Heathrow when two colleagues just missed the chance to connect with each other, because neither knew that the other was there.

SeekYou has no check-ins, no badges, no mayors, no broadcasts to the world: just an efficient, private, permission-based way to maintain your professional connections.
