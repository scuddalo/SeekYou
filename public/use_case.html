USE CASE

SeekYou is a serious professional application designed to keep groups, professional associations, companies and organizations connected. Just two examples:
Alumni Associations can maintain better alumni connections with each other, and the school:

SeekYou opens the door to connect not just one or two alums with each other, but the entire alumni body. Members can rekindle lost connections, make new connections, stay in better touch with existing connections. The entire alumni body is available for contact (depending on the SeekYou privacy settings selected) whether at an alumni event, or a chance meeting when an alum finds herself in, say, Atlanta, and wonders if there’s anyone in town that she might know.
Consulting Firms can keep consultants in better touch with clients, and each other:

Most consulting firms would think that it would be great to know if a key client or colleague was stranded at the same airport…or attending a conference in a neighboring hotel. SeekYou makes it possible. SeekYou enables consultants to look up colleagues and invite clients to meet face-to-face, which helps increase productivity and cement relationships. Suddenly the inevitable airport delays aren’t a waste of time anymore, whatever airport people are in.
Our goal as we designed SeekYou was to keep it simple, secure and fast:

simple enough that it avoids a steep learning curve, secure enough that every user feels in control of his or her privacy and every enterprise is sure that data will never be compromised, and fast enough that professionals – who, let’s face it, have no patience – will use it constantly.
